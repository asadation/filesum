What is FileSum?

I know you've always seen those checksums when downloading any files or software online, and wondered, "What's this for?"

Well, turns out it's a pretty handy thing. You can use it to check the integrity of your downloaded files. However there's a catch, there's always a catch isn't there. Except this one's really not a catch. It's just that the process to verify checksum is a bit involved and not very beginner-friendly. You can do it using the following options:

  • Be a chad and use command-line tools to first calculate the checksum of the downloaded file, then use another tool to compare the two checksums.
  • Download and install an application that can do the both for you.
  • Use this website!

How to use it?

It's way easier than other methods. Just follow the three-step process on the left, and find out the integrity of your file.
